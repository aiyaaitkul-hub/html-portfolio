Thin Milk Pancakes

Description

Thin milk pancakes are soft and light pancakes made with milk, eggs, and flour. From this recipe you can make about 15-20 pancakes, depending on thickness.

Ingredients:

  • Milk - 700 ml
  • Eggs - 2-3
  • Flour - 300 g
  • Sugar - 2-3 tablespoons
  • Salt - 1-2 pinches
  • Vegetable oil - 2-3 tablespoons

Steps:

  1. Break the eggs into a deep bowl.
  2. Add sugar and salt.
  3. Whisk the eggs well, then add milk and mix.
  4. Sift the flour.
  5. Add flour gradually, whisking to avoid lumps.
  6. Add vegetable oil and mix well.
  7. Heat a frying pan and lightly grease it with oil.
  8. Pour the batter into the pan and spread it thinly.
  9. Cook for 1-2 minutes on one side until golden.
  10. Flip and cook the other side for about 30 seconds.
  11. Serve with honey, jam, sour cream, or any filling.

Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Description

Chicken Alfredo Pasta is a creamy Italian dish made with fettuccine, chicken fillet, butter, cream, garlic, and Parmesan cheese. This recipe serves 6 people.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine - 1 pack
  • Chicken fillet - 450 g
  • Heavy cream (33%) - 480 ml
  • Butter (82.5%) - 130 g
  • Parmesan cheese - 90 g
  • Garlic - 3 cloves
  • Olive oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - to taste
  • Parsley - to taste

Steps

  1. Peel and finely chop the garlic.
  2. Wash the chicken fillet, dry it, cut in half, and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil and 1 tablespoon of butter in a pan and fry the chicken until golden on both sides. Remove and keep warm.
  4. Boil water with salt and cook the fettuccine until al dente. Reserve half a cup of pasta water.
  5. In the same pan, melt the remaining butter, add cream and garlic, and heat gently.
  6. Add salt and black pepper, then gradually mix in grated Parmesan cheese until smooth.
  7. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and mix well, adding pasta water if needed.
  8. Slice the chicken fillet and place it on top of the pasta.
  9. Sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve.

Strawberry Ice Cream

Description

Strawberry ice cream is a homemade dessert made with whipped cream, strawberries, and condensed milk. It is creamy, sweet, and perfect for summer. This recipe serves 8 people.

Ingredients

  • Heavy cream (33%) - 500 ml
  • Condensed milk - 100 g
  • Powdered sugar - 2 tablespoons
  • Strawberries - 300 g

Steps

  1. Prepare all ingredients. Use cold heavy cream and fresh or slightly thawed strawberries.
  2. Wash the strawberries, remove stems, and dry them.
  3. Place strawberries in a tall bowl, add powdered sugar, and blend into a puree.
  4. Pour the cold cream into a large bowl and whip with a mixer until thick.
  5. While whipping, add condensed milk and mix well.
  6. Add the strawberry puree to the cream mixture.
  7. Mix everything until smooth and evenly colored.
  8. Transfer the mixture to a container or molds.
  9. Cover and freeze overnight.
  10. Before serving, let the ice cream sit at room temperature for 10 minutes.
  11. Serve with fresh berries or mint if desired.
